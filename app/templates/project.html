    Export for Model Training

    Once you have annotated your text corpus and are ready to train your model, you'll need your language object and a project template that will help to load and pre-process your data and then run model training and packaging. spaCy projects make this process clear and reproducible.

    The final step in your journey will push your data to GitHub where it can be loaded for model training.

    Cadet will automatically:

    • Upload your original texts and generate a datasheet for your dataset.
    • Upload your lookups for lemma, pos and entities.
    • Upload your corpus.json file with frequency data for tokens in the corpus.
    • Upload the bulk updated texts in CoNLL format for annotation with INCEpTION.
    • Finally, we'll serialize and upload your new spaCy language object. We also create a project file for training.
